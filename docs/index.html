How it works

Three steps. Nothing else.

1

Press Control

A short press on the Control key starts recording. A floating bar appears at the bottom of your screen.

2

Speak

Say whatever you want. Live preview text appears as you speak, updating about once per second.

3

Text appears

Press Control again. Your speech is transcribed and pasted right where your cursor was. Done.
